digihabits

Improve your life by adopting good habits

Digihabits aims to facilitate the adoption of good habits by providing a tool for its users to keep track of their progress.

Why?

Our daily choices may seem trivial, but repeated day after day, they help shape the people we are. That's why adopting good lifestyle habits can have an exponential impact on our future.

How does it works?

1

Create a habit and define a recurrence.

2

Log in to the application every day to check off completed completed habits.
